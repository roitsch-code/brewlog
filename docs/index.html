A rabbit hole that started with an NYT article and a befriended coffee expert — and ended with a private brew advisor that gets sharper with every cup. Why it exists →

The app scans a bag, reads your history, and suggests an exact recipe tuned to your equipment, your mood, and your palate — then logs the result to make the next one better. How it works →

It was built in three evenings by one person with a €20 Claude Code account, for no one but himself. Who built it →
